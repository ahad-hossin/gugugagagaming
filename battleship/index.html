    Rules & Accessibility

    How to Play

    1. Deploy your fleet. Ships come as lines and bent hulls (L, T, square) depending on the fleet. Tap a ship then a cell to place it (or drag on desktop). Press R to rotate. Use Auto-Place if you're in a hurry.
    2. Open fire. Click or tap a cell on Enemy Waters to launch. A miss splashes; a hit ignites; sink every enemy ship to win.
    3. Read the board. Markers use shape and colour — a ✕ ring is a miss, a filled burst is a hit, a skull means a ship went down.
    4. Hunt smart. When you land a hit, the ship continues in a straight line. Probe around hits to finish it off.
    5. Make it yours. Four themes, three fleets, three board sizes, and three AI captains — all in Customize.
    6. Use your tech. In ability mode, spend Sonar to scan a 3×3 for contacts, Barrage to bomb a 3×3, or Torpedo to rake a full row/column (R switches row/column). Each costs your turn — use them well.
    7. Play a friend. Choose Play a Friend, share the room code, and battle head-to-head — same device (two tabs) or online. Rematches need both players to agree.

    Keyboard: arrow keys move the cursor, Enter/Space fires or places, R rotates.
